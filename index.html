Volunteering

Giving back to the community and helping those less fortunate is also a big part of who I am!

In June 2023, I volunteered as a food packer for Willing Hearts, an organization that prepares, cooks, and distributes approximately 7,000 daily meals to over 70 locations island-wide, 365 days a year. The beneficiaries include the elderly, disabled, low-income families, children from single-parent families, and migrant workers in Singapore.

In December 2023, I volunteered with Window of Colours, which provides make-up and hair makeovers for the elderly, along with photo-taking services, creating cherished memories and ensuring they have a memorial photo before they pass on.

Currently, I am volunteering in Youth Reading Odyssey, a joint initiative by NUS Community Service Club(CSC) and SHINE Yishun, to help underprivileged children improve their reading skills. Activities include mentoring the children through guided reading and group games. On a deeper level, I also play the role of a dependable big brother, providing emotional support and advice to help them cope with their circumstances.
